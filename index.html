Let's understand the basics...

  • Ramen: Not to be confused with your go-to instant noodles, this is a Japanese noodle soup. It's made of a simple wheat noodle and broth, typically fish or meat-based. It's often garnished with small pieces of meat and vegetables. You can make this fresh or head to any of these restaurants for an authentic hearty bowl of flavour!

  • Ramyeon: This is what you probably see in your local supermarket: The Elite Instant Noodle. Ramyeon is a Korean take on the Japanese traditional and has taken over the world with its salty and spicy flavours! The packet usually contains seasoning powder, dehydrated vegetable flakes and of course, the ramen itself.
